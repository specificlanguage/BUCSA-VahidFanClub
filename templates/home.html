About Us

As Ambassadors, our mission is to serve as a platform for a more collaborative, inclusive, and unified computer science community. We have achieved this by encouraging and fostering a diverse community of students through peer mentorship, community-building events, and support from the computer science clubs. Ultimately, we want to foster and encourage a diverse community but we also want to serve as a social network to qualify the BU CS experience as a whole.

Networking

Through our regular events, community partnerships and various workshop series, students are able to get support from Computer Science clubs at Boston University and expand your opportunities for jobs and internships, and meet other students, faculty and alumni in the Computer Science department.

Resources

In addition to holding events, we also maintain resources to the BU CS community to help students create or improve their resumes, interviews, portfolios, and projects. We also help students find opportunities for students as well as other resources to help Computer Science students at BU succeed throughout their college career.
